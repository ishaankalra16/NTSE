NTSE

NTSE stands for National Talent Search Examination.This exam is to identify and recognise students with high intellect and academic talent at the Secondary School level. It is conducted in two levels:
Stage 1(State Level): Conducted by the States/Union Territories
Stage 2(National Level): Conducted by NCERT
Main objective of this two-tier examination is to identify the talented students who have a special aptitude for Sciences, Mathematics, Social Sciences and questions based on Analytical Reasoning.
